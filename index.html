Our Story

George is super cool. Michelle saw him and was like damn. Now they are getting married. Neat.

George and Michelle met at a bar in what was once known as the Ballston Mall, and is now a construction pit. While George was walking Scout on a rainy Saturday afternoon, he received a not so unusual text invite to grab lunch at the bar. Little did he know, like everywhere else in DC, the bar was filled with people from Michigan (a CMU alumni event in this case, which will be important later). Needing people from a cooler state to make a party, he joined in. George and his Michigan friends continued partying and mixing with CMU folks all day. Meanwhile, Michelle and Colleen (you can read about her in the wedding party section) had yet to miss a CMU event since moving to DC and so became drawn to the party and some beer pong with George and Jake (you can read about him in the wedding party section).

The group continued partying back to Old Town Alexandria where they partook in mystery Jamaican Christmas Liquor from Michelle and Colleen's fridge, danced in bars with sticky floors, and generally got up to no good. When George asked for Michelle's number, she happily gave it to him, but was nearly certain she would be ghosted like Casper. That was not the case and several years later the two would be taking Scout to their new apartment in DC.

Last year, for Michelle's birthday, the two took a trip to Richmond, staying at the storied Jefferson Hotel. Little did Michelle know that George had been carrying an engagement ring loose in his pocket for the last several weeks waiting for the right moment. While relaxing in the pool, George decided it was time and asked Michelle if she wanted to marry him. "Duh". He slipped a ring on her finger under the water while Michelle struggled to figure out what was happening. "Shut up. Shut up. Shut up. Shut up." Now they're getting married, but you know that or you wouldn't be on this website.
